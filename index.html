Abstract

This web site contains some English vocabulary for GRE.

Preface

A good English vocabulary is immensely important for the GRE examination. Learning and keeping track of words is difficult, so the author, Harsh Kapadia, is using this web site as a track of the words that he is learning.

This web site is a work in progress and is constantly updated! The web site source code can be found on GitHub. Do not hesitate to raise an issue if there is any mistake/typo in the web site. Inputs and suggestions are appreciated and most welcome!

The author, Harsh Kapadia, can be contacted via LinkedIn, Twitter or e-mail (harshgkapadia@gmail.com).

A

Abhor

Meaning

  • Strong dislike or hate for something or someone.

Sentence

  • Drugs are abhorred by most people because of the destructive addiction that it causes.

Agnostic

Meaning

  • Someone who does not know or believes that it is impossible to know if God exists.

Sentence

  • An agnostic person can be both, an atheist or a theist.

Altruist

Meaning

  • An unselfish person who thinks of the need of others.

Synonyms

  • Benevolent

Sentence

  • An altruist always ends up winning in life.

Anthropology

Meaning

  • The study of man.

  • The science of human development and history.

Ascetic

Meaning

  • Living a life of forced hardship and abstinence.

Synonyms

  • Austere

Sentence

  • He lived the life of an ascetic, as he abhorred self-indulgence and luxury.

C

Chauvinist

Meaning

  • Believing that one’s own country or race is the best.

  • Believing that women are naturally less important, intelligent or able than men.

Synonyms

  • Jingoist

Sentence

  • It is a deeply chauvinist community where the few women who have jobs are ridiculed.

Conceited

Meaning

  • Too much pride in oneself.

Sentence

  • Being humble and working hard is the way to succeed in life, so conceitedness will lead one nowhere.

Condescending

Meaning

  • Thinking that one is superior than the others.

Synonyms

  • Supercilious

Sentence

  • Being condescending to juniors or less fortunate is never good, as one can learn something from every person.

E

Effete

Meaning

  • Degenerate, worn out and weakened.

  • Sterile

Sentence

  • The entire family was effeted after the long travel.

Epicure

Meaning

  • One who enjoys high quality food and drinks.

Note
 Do not confuse epicure with epicurean.

Sentence

  • Chefs are epicures.

Epicurean

Meaning

  • A person believing that pleasure, happiness and luxury is the chief good.

Note
 Do not confuse epicurean with epicure.

Sentence

  • Epicureans are usually jolted back to the harsh reality of life.

Expiate

Meaning

  • To make amends for something.

Synonyms

  • Atone

Sentence

  • One should try to expiate their sins and mistakes.

F

Foibles

Meaning

  • A strange, unimportant and unharming habit.

Synonyms

  • Idiosyncracies

Sentence

  • We all have our own foibles.

G

Gregarious

Meaning

  • Talkative

  • Extrovert

  • Social

Sentence

  • No one doubts the greagariousness of human beings.

I

Importune

Meaning

  • Beg for something ceaselessly.

Synonyms

  • Beseech

Sentence

  • The poor man importuned the bank employee for a loan.

Impute

Meaning

  • Attribute a bad habit or act to someone.

Sentence

  • The plaintiff imputed the defendant.

Indolent

Meaning

  • Lazy

Sentence

  • 'Indolent hustle' is an oxymoron.

Inundate

Meaning

  • To give someone too much work to handle.

  • To flood an area.

Sentence

  • The callcenter was inundated with people asking for technical support during the COVID-19 pandemic.

  • The river overflowed in the monsoon and inundated the surrounding villages.

L

Liberal

Meaning

  • In excess

  • People who support new, different, progressive, explorative and experimental policies.

Sentence

  • Do not forget to apply sunscreen liberally in Egypt to avoid sunburns!

Related

  • Radical

Ludicrous

Meaning

  • Laughably stupid

  • Unreasonable

Synonyms

  • Ridiculous

  • Preposterous

  • Inane

Sentence

  • Chauvinists go to ludicrous extremes to justify themselves.

M

Maudlin

Meaning

  • A person who cries easily and without much reason.

  • Over sentimental

  • Drinking can lead to this as well.

Sentence

  • In inebriated states, some people become maudlin.

Misanthrope

Meaning

  • One who hates man/mankind.

Sentence

  • Humans are social creatures by nature, so misanthropes are quite rare.

Misogamy

Meaning

  • Hatred of marriages.

Monocle

Meaning

  • A lens for one eye.

Sentence

  • German Generals used to wear monocles.

Mulct

Meaning

  • Taking unjust advantage of someone.

Sentence

  • One should not mulct someone who is being nice to them.

O

Obsequious

Meaning

  • Insincerely servile in expectance of reward

Synonym

  • Fawning

Sentence

  • If a waiter at a restaurant realises that you will tip them well, they will act obsequiously with you by being extra polite and attentive.

Ostracize

Meaning

  • Isolation by society.

  • Being avoided intentionally by someone.

Synonyms

  • Outcast

Sentence

  • It is incorrect to ostracize people of the LGBTQIA+ community, because 'Love is love'!

P

Pander

Meaning

  • Cater to/appeal to certain people or emotions/desires.

Sentence

  • Dishonest politicians generally try to pander to the greed and demands of the mob to gain power.

Philology

Meaning

  • The study of language.

  • The science of liguistics.

Plaintiff

Meaning

  • The person who files a legal action against someone, ie, the defendant, accusing them of wrongdoings.

Sentence

  • The plaintiff was a very upright and honest man.

Podiatrist

Meaning

  • A Doctor who specializes in treating ailments of human feet.

Synonyms

  • Chiropodist

Sentence

  • A podiatrist can treat a corn on your feet.

Polygot

Meaning

  • One who can speak many languages.

Sentence

  • Being a polygot allows one to interact with a wider range of people, thus gathering a lot of perspectives on various issues.

R

Radical

Meaning

  • An extreme liberal.

Sentence

  • Jihadis have a radical thinking.

Rationalize

Meaning

  • Justifying a wrong or selfish act.

Sentence

  • Own up to your mistakes. Don’t rationalize them.

Rusticate

Meaning

  • Expel from a school or college.

  • To go to the countryside.

Sentences

  • Shannon’s bad behaviour got her rusticated from the college.

  • The Kjellberg’s rusticate to the country in the Summer.

S

Scintillate

Meaning

  • Sparkle with wit or humour.

Sentence

  • Camila found herself attracted to Lauren because of her scintillating personality.

Sublimate

Meaning

  • Divert destructive energy and actions to something constructive, usually unconsciously.

Sentence

  • It takes self-awareness and acceptance to identify destructive patterns in oneself and sublimate them into some productive task.

T

Theology

Meaning

  • The study of God and religion.

Sentence

  • An atheist will never become a theologist.

V

Vegetate

Meaning

  • A passive and monotonous living.

Sentence

  • Instead of utilizing her drop year from college, Fletcher has been vegetating.

Vicarious

Meaning

  • Living through someone else.

  • Experiencing something indirectly.

Sentence

  • It is the truth that an avid reader vicariously lives a thousand lives!

W

Wanton

Meaning

  • Careless disregard for something

Sentence

  • Some party-goers are wanton by being lewd and not caring about the next day’s hangover.

Kindness always wins.
— Selena Gomez
