Abstract

This web site contains some English vocabulary for GRE.

Preface

A good English vocabulary is immensely important for the GRE examination. Learning and keeping track of words is difficult, so the author, Harsh Kapadia, is using this web site as a track of the words that he is learning.

This web site is a work in progress and is constantly updated! The web site source code can be found on GitHub. Do not hesitate to raise an issue if there is any mistake/typo in the web site. Inputs and suggestions are appreciated and most welcome!

The author, Harsh Kapadia, can be contacted via Twitter, LinkedIn or e-mail (harshgkapadia@gmail.com).

A

Abhor

Meaning

  • Strong dislike or hate for something or someone.

Sentence

  • Drugs are abhorred by most people because of the destructive addiction that it causes.

Acrophobia

Meaning

  • A fear of heights.

Sentence

  • Bunjee Jumping is an extremely scary activity for acrophobic people.

Adonis

Meaning

  • A good looking man who makes others attracted to him.

Sentence

  • Logan is an adonis, but he is not that great as a person.

Agnostic

Meaning

  • Someone who does not know or believes that it is impossible to know if God exists.

Sentence

  • An agnostic person can be both, an atheist or a theist.

Agoraphobia

Meaning

  • Fear of open spaces or lonely places.

This is the opposite/antonym of Claustrophobia.

Sentence

  • Being in a deserted place brings out the agoraphobic side of her.

Altruist

Meaning

  • An unselfish person who thinks of the need of others.

Synonyms

  • Benevolent

Sentence

  • An altruist always ends up winning in life.

Anthropology

Meaning

  • The study of man.

  • The science of human development and history.

Ascetic

Meaning

  • Living a life of forced hardship and abstinence.

Synonyms

  • Austere

  • Puritanical

Sentence

  • He lived the life of an ascetic, as he abhorred self-indulgence and luxury.

B

Barroom

Meaning

  • An establishment whose main business is a bar for the sale of liquor.

Sentence

  • Barroom brawls are not very common.

C

Chauvinist

Meaning

  • Believing that one’s own country or race is the best.

  • Believing that women are naturally less important, intelligent or able than men.

Synonyms

  • Jingoist

Sentence

  • It is a deeply chauvinist community where the few women who have jobs are ridiculed.

Circe

Meaning

  • A woman who derives pleasure from leading men to their destruction.

Sentence

  • Lauren let down her hair and Camila accused her of being like Circe, who turned men into brute beasts and would not let them feel love.

Clairvoyant

Meaning

  • A person who claims to be able to see things not visible to ordinary people.

  • They make prophecies.

Sentence

  • It is debatable whether people can be clairvoyants.

Claustrophobia

Meaning

  • The fear of closed (confined) spaces or crowded places.

This is the opposite/antonym of Agoraphobia.

Sentence

  • She is so claustrophobic that she uses the stairs in a building rather than the lift to go up and down.

Conceited

Meaning

  • Too much pride in oneself.

Sentence

  • Being humble and working hard is the way to succeed in life, so conceitedness will lead one nowhere.

Condescending

Meaning

  • Thinking that one is superior than the others.

Synonyms

  • Supercilious

Sentence

  • Being condescending to juniors or less fortunate is never good, as one can learn something from every person.

Connoisseur

Meaning

  • A person who has a lot of knowledge in arts, food, women, drinks, etc…​

  • A person with a fine taste.

Synonyms

  • Virtuoso

Sentence

  • He looked like the stereotypical connoisseur at the art gallery in his formal coat with a wine glass in his hand.

Coquette

Meaning

  • A girl who flirts a lot.

  • A girl who promises much, but delivers very little.

D

Demagogue

Meaning

  • Pandering to the hatered and prejudices of people to satisfy one’s ulterior motives.

Sentence

  • Certain demagogue political parties create a chaos in the society by increasing friction between religious communities to de-stabilize the political party in power.

Demur

Meaning

  • Show reluctance.

  • Hesitate

Do not confuse 'demur' with 'demure'.

Sentence

  • "There isn’t any too much room in this bungalow as it is," demurred Mr. Pottle.

Demure

Meaning

  • Shy

  • Docile

  • Modest

  • Reserved

Do not confuse 'demure' with 'demur'.

Sentence

  • Introverted people are demure.

Dipsomaniac

Meaning

  • A person who is addicted to drinking intoxicating substances like alcohol.

E

Effete

Meaning

  • Degenerate, worn out and weakened.

  • Sterile

Sentence

  • The entire family was effeted after the long travel.

Egregious

Meaning

  • A grave and blatant/glaring error.

Synonyms

  • Flagrant

Sentence

  • Any mistake by oneself, whether inconspicuous or egregious, should be accepted and atoned for in a genuine manner. Honesty is appreciated by people and they do forgive one’s mistakes.

Epicure

Meaning

  • One who enjoys high quality food and drinks.

Do not confuse epicure with epicurean.

Sentence

  • Chefs are epicures.

Epicurean

Meaning

  • A person believing that pleasure, happiness and luxury is the chief good.

Do not confuse epicurean with epicure.

Sentence

  • Epicureans are usually jolted back to the harsh reality of life.

Expatriate

Meaning

  • Someone who does not live in their own country.

Sentence

  • Migrants are expatriates.

Expiate

Meaning

  • To make amends for something.

Synonyms

  • Atone

Sentence

  • One should try to expiate their sins and mistakes.

Eschew

Meaning

  • Stop doing something.

  • Avoid intentionally.

Synonyms

  • Abrogate

Sentence

  • India told Pakistan that there would be no talks between them until they eschewed their terror activities and ceasefire violations.

Esthete

Meaning

  • A person of fine and artistic flair.

  • A person who is attracted to beautiful things.

Sentence

  • Knowing how genuine Marzia Kjellberg is and judging from her videos, clothing line and Instagram Stories, one can glean that she is an esthete person.

Expound

Meaning

  • Explain in detail.

Synonyms

  • Elaborate

Sentences

  • They have an annoying habit of expounding on unnecessary things.

F

Fascism

Meaning

  • A political system based on a very powerful leader, state control, and being extremely proud of country and race, and in which political opposition is not allowed. (Source)

Sentence

  • A fascist leader is a chauvinist and a dictator.

Fastidious

Meaning

  • One who pays a lot much attention to details.

  • Perfectionist

Synonyms

  • Pedant

Sentence

  • Being fastidious can be detrimental to one’s progress.

Foibles

Meaning

  • A strange, unimportant and unharming habit.

Synonyms

  • Idiosyncracies

Sentence

  • We all have our own foibles.

Futilitarian

Meaning

  • Pessimist

  • Cynic

Sentence

  • A futilitarian attitude will get one nowhere in life.

G

Gregarious

Meaning

  • Talkative

  • Extrovert

  • Social

Sentence

  • No one doubts the greagariousness of human beings.

I

Importune

Meaning

  • Beg for something ceaselessly.

Synonyms

  • Beseech

Sentence

  • The poor man importuned the bank employee for a loan.

Imbue

Meaning

  • Fill something or someone with a particular feeling, quality or idea.

Sentence

  • Cuddling imbues one with a comforting and soothing feeling.

Impute

Meaning

  • Attribute a bad habit or act to someone.

Sentence

  • The plaintiff imputed the defendant.

Indolent

Meaning

  • Lazy

Sentence

  • 'Indolent hustle' is an oxymoron.

Inundate

Meaning

  • To give someone too much work to handle.

  • To flood an area.

Sentence

  • The callcenter was inundated with people asking for technical support during the COVID-19 pandemic.

  • The river overflowed in the monsoon and inundated the surrounding villages.

J

Jingoist

Meaning

  • Believing that one’s country is the best, often shown in enthusiastic support for a war against another country.

Synonyms

  • Chauvinist

Sentence

  • A jingoist and a chauvinist can be the best of friends.

Judas

Meaning

  • A person who can sell out someone close to them for money.

Sentence

  • A Judas will always end up unhappy in life, because money is not the panacea for everything.

K

Kleptomaniac

Meaning

  • A person with a compulsion to steal, no matter what their financial condition is.

Sentence

  • Kleptomania affects her so much that she steals small trinkets, plants and other insignificant things from the places she visits.

L

Liberal

Meaning

  • In excess

  • People who support new, different, progressive, explorative and experimental policies.

Sentence

  • Do not forget to apply sunscreen liberally in Egypt to avoid sunburns!

Related

  • Radical

Ludicrous

Meaning

  • Laughably stupid

  • Unreasonable

Synonyms

  • Ridiculous

  • Preposterous

  • Inane

Sentence

  • Chauvinists go to ludicrous extremes to justify themselves.

M

Maudlin

Meaning

  • A person who cries easily and without much reason.

  • Over sentimental

  • Drinking can lead to this as well.

Sentence

  • In inebriated states, some people become maudlin.

Martinet

Meaning

  • A fanatic of following discipline.

  • Stickler for form and ettiquette.

Synonyms

  • Punctilious

  • Pedant

Meritocracy

Meaning

  • A system in which people are moved into power or are accepted into something based on their merit and abilities.

Sentence

  • All systems in today’s world claim to be meritocratic, but with 'correct' contacts and backdoor dealings, is that the case?

Misanthrope

Meaning

  • One who hates man/mankind.

Sentence

  • Humans are social creatures by nature, so misanthropes are quite rare.

Misogamy

Meaning

  • Hatred of marriages.

Monocle

Meaning

  • A lens for one eye.

Sentence

  • German Generals used to wear monocles.

Mulct

Meaning

  • Taking unjust advantage of someone.

Synonyms

  • Exploit

Sentence

  • One should not mulct someone who is being nice to them.

O

Obsequious

Meaning

  • Insincerely servile in expectance of reward

Synonym

  • Fawning

  • Sycophant

Sentence

  • If a waiter at a restaurant realises that you will tip them well, they will act obsequiously with you by being extra polite and attentive.

Ostentatious

Meaning

  • Showing off money, power or possessions to seek attention and appreciation.

Synonyms

  • Flamboyant

  • Flashy

Sentence

  • A pedant is ostentatious about their learning.

Ostracize

Meaning

  • Isolation by society.

  • Being avoided intentionally by someone.

Synonyms

  • Outcast

Sentence

  • It is incorrect to ostracize people of the LGBTQIA+ community, because 'Love is love'!

P

Pander

Meaning

  • Cater to/appeal to certain people or emotions/desires.

Sentence

  • Dishonest politicians generally try to pander to the greed and demands of the mob to gain power.

Pedant

Meaning

  • Shows off knowledge.

  • Unnecessarily corrects petty mistakes.

Synonyms

  • Fastidious

Sentence

  • A less knowledgeable but humble person is better than a pedant.

Philatelist

Meaning

  • A postage stamp collector.

Philology

Meaning

  • The study of language.

  • The science of liguistics.

Plaintiff

Meaning

  • The person who files a legal action against someone, ie, the defendant, accusing them of wrongdoings.

Sentence

  • The plaintiff was a very upright and honest man.

Podiatrist

Meaning

  • A Doctor who specializes in treating ailments of human feet.

Synonyms

  • Chiropodist

Sentence

  • A podiatrist can treat a corn on your feet.

Polygot

Meaning

  • One who can speak many languages.

Sentence

  • Being a polygot allows one to interact with a wider range of people, thus gathering a lot of perspectives on various issues.

Prudent

Meaning

  • Shrewd

  • Careful

  • Avoiding risks

  • Cautious

Sentence

  • It is prudent to read any contract thoroughly and get a lawyer to go through it as well.

Puerile

Meaning

  • Silly, immature or childish behaviour.

Sentence

  • I find his sense of humour rather puerile.

Puritanical

Meaning

  • Not believing in pleasure, but only in working hard and practising self-discipline.

Synonyms

  • Ascetic

Sentence

  • He is very puritanical about sex.

R

Radical

Meaning

  • An extreme liberal.

Sentence

  • Jihadis have a radical thinking.

Rationalize

Meaning

  • Justifying a wrong or selfish act.

Sentence

  • Own up to your mistakes. Don’t rationalize them.

Repertoire

Meaning

  • A stack of expertise that can be offered.

  • A list of dramas or music pieces that can be performed by a person or a group.

Sentence

  • The Royal Shakespeare Company also have many modern plays in their repertoire.

Rusticate

Meaning

  • Expel from a school or college.

  • To go to the countryside.

Sentences

  • Shannon’s bad behaviour got her rusticated from the college.

  • The Kjellberg’s rusticate to the country in the Summer.

S

Scintillate

Meaning

  • Sparkle with wit or humour.

Sentence

  • Camila found herself attracted to Lauren because of her scintillating personality.

Sublimate

Meaning

  • Divert destructive energy and actions to something constructive, usually unconsciously.

Sentence

  • It takes self-awareness and acceptance to identify destructive patterns in oneself and sublimate them into some productive task.

Sycophant

Meaning

  • Insincerely servile.

Synonyms

  • Obsequious

T

Theology

Meaning

  • The study of God and religion.

Sentence

  • An atheist will never become a theologist.

Tyro

Meaning

  • A beginner.

Sentence

  • One should never make fun of a tyro, as everyone starts out as a beginner.

U

Uncouth

Meaning

  • Unpleasant

  • Rude

Sentence

  • Viragoes are uncouth.

Untenable

Meaning

  • A theory, argument or situation that cannot be defended.

Sentence

  • Once Harish Salve produced the evidence in front of the court, the defendants arguments became untenable.

V

Vegetate

Meaning

  • A passive and monotonous living.

Sentence

  • Instead of utilizing her drop year from college, Fletcher has been vegetating.

Vicarious

Meaning

  • Living through someone else.

  • Experiencing something indirectly.

Sentence

  • It is the truth that an avid reader vicariously lives a thousand lives!

Virago

Meaning

  • A vicious, nagging and battle-axe type woman.

Sentence

  • Mothers who are viragos usually have docile children.

Virtuoso

Meaning

  • A person skilled at something, especially when it comes to the fine arts like music, painting, etc…​

Synonyms

  • Connoisseur

Sentence

  • It is important for a virtuoso to not be a pedant.

W

Wanton

Meaning

  • Careless disregard for something

Sentence

  • Some party-goers are wanton by being lewd and not caring about the next day’s hangover.
