=== Sanguine

==== Meaning

* Confidently optimistic.
* Marked by eager hopefulness.
* Consisting of or relating to blood.

==== Synonyms

* Bloodred
* Bloodthirsty
* Murderous
* Homicidal

==== Sentences

* He has been strangely [.underline]#sanguine# about this, blandly ignoring the mounting evidence.
