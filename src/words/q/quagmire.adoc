=== Quagmire

==== Meaning

* A difficult, precarious or entrapping position.
* Soft miry land that shakes or yields under the foot.

==== Synonyms

* Predicament
* Impasse
* Dilemma
* Pickle
* Swamp
* Marsh

==== Sentences

* Securing adequate international health insurance, for example, became a bureaucratic [.underline]#quagmire#.
