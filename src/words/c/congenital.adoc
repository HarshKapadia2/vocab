=== Congenital

==== Meaning

* Something that is existing at or dating from birth.
* Being such by nature.

NOTE: Do not confuse 'congenital' with 'link:#_congenial[congenial]'.

==== Synonyms

* Hereditary
* Innate
* Inborn

==== Sentences

* Brea had a [.underline]#congenital# heart disease.
